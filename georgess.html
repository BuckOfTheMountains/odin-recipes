Ingredients

  • 14oz of Katz's Deli Pastrami
  • Two thick slices of seeded marble rye
  • 4 slices of swiss cheese
  • Hot German mustrad to taste
  • 1 small red and green cabbage
  • 1 small endive and radiccico
  • 1.5 cups Shredded carrorts
  • 1.5 cups of shredded celery
  • 6 Thinly shredded green onions
  • 1 small red bell pepper diced
  • 1 large jalapeno diced
  • 1 habanero diced
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 1/3 cup ranch dressing
  • Celery salt, to taste
  • Lemon pepper, to taste
  • Parsley and cilantro, to taste
  • Garlic powder, dash, optional

Directions

  • 1. Heat a griddle, butter the seeded marble rye, Schnitzer's brand is prefered especially if taken off of a fishing hook 4 stories in the air. Get it toasted but not burnt.
  • 2. Add hot German mustard on one side, layer on two slices of cheese, then the Kat's pastrami, followed by the additional slices of cheese.
  • 3. In a large mixing bowl, one that would comfortably fit a big salad, assemble the slaw in the following order; thinly slice the green and red cabbages, the endive and radiccico, green onions and incorporate. Mix in the carrots and celery, followed by the thinly diced red bell pepper, large jalapeno and habanero. Incorporate the ranch dressing and lime juice. Season with celery salt and lemon pepper to taste, follwed by the parsley and cilantro if using, a dash of garlic powder never hurts. Add slaw to the bottom part of the sandwhich and assemble. Or have on the side.
  • 4. Hide sandwhich in a bedroom end table just before making love. Procede to make love and snack intermittently. Pairs well with cold Pepsi. If all fails, head to the diner for a plate of chicken salad on rye, untoasted, with a cup of tea.
